## The product

An anonymous 21-day writing ritual for Indian college students. One matched stranger from another college. One honest note each night, sealed until midnight IST. On Day 21, both independently choose whether to reveal. The anonymity is the product. The reveal is the payoff.

## The sacred list

1. **Anonymity first.** No identity surfaces before a mutual Day-21 reveal. One "stay anonymous" keeps both private.
2. **The seal is server-enforced.** Seal state, unlock state, day counter, and reveal mutuality are computed on the server only. Clients render; they never decide.
3. **No streaks, no gamification, no badges, no points.** Missed nights are grace, not failure.
4. **No feeds.** The Silent Room is one anonymous line per night, no replies, no likes, gone in 7 days, cohort-scoped.
5. **No astrology.** The night sky is atmosphere, never mechanics.
6. **No AI inside the pair, ever.** No AI replies, companions, summaries, or writing assistance. The test for any AI proposal: does it replace a human's presence or protect it? Replacers are rejected without debate.
7. **The four archetypes:** Protector, Connector, Performer, Disconnector. No variants.
8. **Safety is never monetized, gated, or removed:** report, block, rematch, crisis resources. Also never monetized: the reveal itself, and basic anonymity.
9. **18+ only.**
10. **The voice is a quiet 3am friend.** Banned everywhere: sanctuary, oracle, celestial, safe space, holistic, wellness, streak. Never cheerful by default, never clinical, never corporate.
11. **No user data is ever sold or sent to tracking SDKs.** Analytics are first-party events only. The privacy policy is a promise, not copy.
12. **Honesty over polish.** No invented statistics, no fake testimonials, no simulated security theater, no claims the code does not perform.
